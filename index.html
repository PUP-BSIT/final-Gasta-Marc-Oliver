Reason for Face to Face

I think the reason for the f-t-f class is because of the cruciality of the lesson. It might be hard for us to learn it if we did an online class. For me, online class can be abused in a lot of ways. As the teacher can't see us or observe us during the session. On the other hand, it is helpful especially to those persons who live far from the campus. Personally, I prefer face to face classes in this subject because I think I learn more if it's face to face rather than online classes. As in online classes I can be very easily distracted by other things.
